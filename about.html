Our Story

Built out of frustration with the status quo

Most local businesses get one of two things when they go looking for a website — a $200 Wix template that looks like every other site on the block, or a $15,000 agency quote that takes four months and still misses the mark.

TadCore was built to be the third option. Fast, premium, and actually built for the industry you're in. We don't recycle designs. We don't hand you a login to a drag-and-drop builder and call it done. We build real websites that represent your business the way it deserves to be represented.

We work exclusively with local businesses across Los Angeles — the kind of businesses where a sharp website directly translates into more phone calls, more bookings, and more clients walking through the door.

"Most local businesses don't have a website problem — they have a first impression problem."

The average person decides whether to trust a business within the first 7 seconds of landing on their site. We build for those 7 seconds — and everything that comes after.

What We Stand For

The principles we build by

01
Quality over volume

We don't take on every project that comes through the door. We take on the ones we can do exceptionally well. Every client gets our full attention.

02
Honesty over hype

We tell you what your site needs, not what sounds impressive. If something won't move the needle for your business, we won't charge you for it.

Beautiful means nothing if it doesn't convert. Every design decision we make is tied back to one question — does this get the visitor to take action?
